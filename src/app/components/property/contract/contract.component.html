Parties: This Lease Agreement ("Lease") is made on May 29, 2024, by and between {{landlord.firstName}} {{landlord.lastName}} ("Landlord") and {{buyerFirstName}} ("Tenant").

Property Description: The residential property located at 456 Elm Street, Apt 2B, Springfield, IL 62704.

Term of Lease: The term of this Lease shall commence on June 1, 2024, and end on May 31, 2025.

Rent Payment: The monthly rent shall be $1,200, payable on the first day of each month. Late fees of $50 apply if rent is not received by the 5th day of the month.

Security Deposit: The Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $1,200 upon signing this Lease. The deposit will be returned within 30 days of Lease termination, less any deductions for damages or unpaid rent.

Utilities and Services: The rent includes water and trash removal. The Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, and internet services.

Maintenance and Repairs: The Tenant is responsible for maintaining the property in good condition and must report any needed repairs to the Landlord promptly. The Landlord is responsible for major repairs and maintenance.

Use of Premises: The property shall be used for residential purposes only. The Tenant shall not engage in any illegal activities or sublet the property without written consent from the Landlord.

Occupancy Limits: The property shall be occupied by no more than 2 adults and 1 child.

Pets: No pets are allowed without prior written consent from the Landlord. If permitted, a pet deposit of $300 is required.

Rules and Regulations: The Tenant agrees to abide by all house rules and regulations, which are attached to this Lease.

Alterations and Improvements: The Tenant may not make any alterations or improvements without the Landlord's written consent.

Access to Premises: The Landlord may enter the property with 24-hour notice for inspections, repairs, or emergencies.

Insurance: The Tenant is required to maintain renters insurance for personal property and liability coverage.

Termination of Lease: The Tenant may terminate this Lease with 30 days' written notice. The Landlord may terminate this Lease for cause with 30 days' written notice or immediately for non-payment of rent or other serious violations.

Default and Remedies: Failure to pay rent or comply with Lease terms constitutes default. The Landlord may seek legal remedies, including eviction and collection of unpaid rent.

Miscellaneous Provisions: This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois. Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
